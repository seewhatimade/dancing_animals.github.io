# Dancing Animals!

https://github.com/seewhatimade/dancing_animals.github.io/

## How to Play

Tap any animal to make it dance! Each animal has its own dance style and plays a musical note when tapped.

- **10 animals** to tap: Cat, Dog, Cow, Pig, Bunny, Frog, Fox, Penguin, Lion, Bear
- **5 dance styles**: bounce, wiggle, sway, spin, and hop
- **Music starts** when you tap your first animal — a fun beat plays in the background
- **Sparkles and music notes** fly out of dancing animals
- **"Dance All!" button** (top-right) makes every animal dance at once
- **Disco ball** at the top lights up with colors when animals are dancing
- **Disco floor** tiles pulse with rainbow colors to the beat

No rules, no score — just tap and enjoy the show!
